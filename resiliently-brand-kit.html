Production Rules
Non-Negotiable
01
Bebas Neue always in caps
Every headline, every stat, every label in Bebas Neue is uppercase. No exceptions.
02
Caveat always lowercase
Handwritten annotations are always lowercase. The contrast between CAPS machine and lowercase human IS the brand.
03
Two colors: blue and red
Blue (#5ec1ff) is the accent for everything positive — stats, checkmarks, annotations, highlights. Red (#eb0000) is for danger only — exclusions, denials, warnings. Nothing else is colored.
04
Black canvas, never flat
#000000 background. Add depth with #0a0a0a cards and #1a1a1a surfaces. Never use a colored background.
05
Four fonts, four roles
Bebas Neue = headlines. Caveat = handwritten. DM Sans = body. JetBrains Mono = data. Never cross roles. Never add a fifth.
06
No rounded corners
Sharp edges everywhere. 0px radius. Editorial sharpness, not startup softness.
07
German-first on-screen
All motion graphics text in German. English only for Pillar B code demos.
08
No company names
Never show Zurich, AXA, Allianz, or real client names. Fictitious scenarios only.
