Adlery / Another Shabby — not in Zetafonts
Adlery Pro (by Seniors Studio) — casual brush script, very readable, popular on YouTube thumbnails. Available on Creative Market or MyFonts (~$30).

Another Shabby (by Emyln Studio) — messy casual handwritten, very natural. Also on Creative Market / MyFonts (~$20).

If you want either of these, buy them and drop the .woff2 into the fonts/ folder. They slot directly into any of the pairings above as the handwritten accent — same structure, just swap the script font.
